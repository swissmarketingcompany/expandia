Questions
and Answers

What do we need to provide?

A target location, target customer type, and any offer rules you want reflected in the proposal.

Can you prepare both residential and commercial offers?

Yes. B2C offers focus on homeowners. B2B offers include business details, contact research, and commercial context.

Are the roof images real?

The roof research uses real available imagery. PV visuals are AI-assisted previews created for proposal use.

How do you know how many solar panels to add?

By default, we add the minimum amount so the offer looks acceptable, but we can adapt to any strategy you want.

Is every offer reviewed by a person?

Yes. Human review is included before the offer batch is delivered.

Can we start with one small region?

Yes. A first batch can focus on one municipality, town, postal area, or sales territory.

Do you offer discounts for larger batches?

Yes. Larger batches and recurring work can receive discounted pricing.

Can pricing be success-based?

Yes, but as a discount. There will still be a price per offer.

Can the offer format match our sales process?

Yes. The offer structure, assumptions, visuals, and review rules can be adapted to your preferred sales approach.

Can you prepare offers for areas where we already sell?

Yes. You can choose the areas where your team already installs, services, or wants more coverage.
