Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our CRM management and optimization services.

How long does a CRM audit take?

A typical comprehensive audit takes 3-5 business days. Our process covers:

  • Data Structure Analysis: Field usage, custom properties, and data integrity
  • Automation Review: Workflow efficiency, triggers, and redundancy checks
  • User Adoption Audit: How your team actually uses the CRM vs. best practices
  • Integration Health: Sync status, API connections, and data flow validation
  • Reporting Assessment: Dashboard accuracy and executive visibility gaps

Can you fix messy data without deleting important records?

Absolutely. We use advanced deduplication and merging strategies that preserve all historical activity and notes while cleaning up your database. Our data hygiene process includes:

Step Action Protection
1. Backup Full database export Complete rollback capability
2. Deduplication Merge duplicate records All activities preserved
3. Standardization Normalize formatting Original data archived
4. Enrichment Fill missing fields Verified sources only

Do you build custom automations?

Yes, we build custom workflows tailored to your specific sales process:

  • Lead Routing: Automatic assignment based on territory, deal size, or product line
  • Deal Stage Alerts: Real-time notifications when deals stall or progress
  • Renewal Reminders: Automated sequences for contract renewals and upsells
  • Cross-Object Updates: Keep related records in sync across your entire CRM
  • Custom Reporting: Automated reports delivered to stakeholders on schedule

Do you offer training for our team?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training and documentation to ensure your team maximizes CRM adoption:

  • Recorded Sessions: Video walkthroughs of every workflow and dashboard
  • Written Documentation: Step-by-step guide customized to your CRM setup
  • Live Q&A: Interactive sessions for your sales and ops teams
  • Ongoing Support: Post-launch assistance to address questions as they arise
