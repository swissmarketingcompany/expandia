Common Questions

What companies usually ask once they see the demos.

Are these real products? +

These are small live demos to show the kind of microapps we can build. The client version would be shaped around real business rules and real data.

Can you connect these apps to our systems? +

Yes. The useful version is usually the connected version, not the standalone demo version.

Do we need a big platform first? +

No. Many companies start with one small app that solves one clear problem.

Can these be internal tools only? +

Yes. Many of the best first apps are internal tools for finance, sales, legal, operations, or support teams.
