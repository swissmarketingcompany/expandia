### Understanding Machine Learning

**What is Machine Learning?**
Machine Learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables computers to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. ML systems automatically identify patterns in data and make predictions or decisions.

**Types of Machine Learning:**

1. **Supervised Learning**
   - Uses labeled training data
   - Predicts outcomes for new data
   - Examples: Email spam detection, sales forecasting

2. **Unsupervised Learning**
   - Finds patterns in unlabeled data
   - Discovers hidden structures
   - Examples: Customer segmentation, anomaly detection

3. **Reinforcement Learning**
   - Learns through trial and error
   - Optimizes actions for maximum reward
   - Examples: Game playing, autonomous vehicles
