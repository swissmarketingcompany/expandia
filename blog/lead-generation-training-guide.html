‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What should be included in lead generation training?

Comprehensive lead generation training should include: prospecting techniques and tools, qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC), communication skills (cold calling, email outreach), social selling strategies, CRM and technology usage, objection handling, data analysis and reporting, and industry-specific knowledge relevant to your target market.

How long does effective lead generation training take?

Initial training typically takes 2-4 weeks for comprehensive coverage, followed by 3-6 months of ongoing coaching and reinforcement. Skills mastery requires 6-12 months with consistent practice. Advanced techniques and specialization can take 12-18 months depending on complexity and industry requirements.

How do you measure lead generation training effectiveness?

Key metrics include: Activity levels (calls, emails, meetings), conversion rates at each stage, lead quality scores, pipeline generation, revenue attribution, skill assessment scores, time to productivity for new hires, and team performance improvements over time. Regular assessments and feedback cycles ensure continuous improvement.

What's the typical ROI of lead generation training?

Most organizations see 300-600% ROI within 12 months through improved conversion rates, increased productivity, and reduced turnover. Top performers achieve 800%+ ROI by combining training with ongoing coaching and process optimization. Investment typically pays back within 3-6 months through enhanced performance.
