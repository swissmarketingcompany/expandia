‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is a sales development agency?

A sales development agency is a specialized company that provides outsourced sales development representatives (SDRs) and related services. They handle prospecting, lead qualification, appointment setting, and pipeline generation activities to help companies scale their sales operations without hiring internal SDR teams.

How much does a sales development agency cost?

Sales development agency costs typically range from $4,000-15,000 per month depending on service level: Basic SDR services ($4,000-8,000/month), Full-service programs ($8,000-15,000/month), Enterprise solutions ($15,000+/month). Most agencies charge a combination of retainer fees plus performance bonuses based on meetings booked or opportunities created.

What should I look for when choosing a sales development agency?

Key factors include: proven track record with measurable results, industry expertise and vertical specialization, quality of SDR talent and training programs, technology stack and CRM integrations, transparent reporting and analytics, cultural fit and communication style, and appropriate pricing models that align with your goals.

How long does it take to see results from an SDR agency?

Most reputable SDR agencies deliver initial results within 2-4 weeks: Week 1-2 (setup and onboarding), Week 3-4 (first qualified meetings), Month 2-3 (optimization and scaling). Full optimization typically takes 3-6 months to achieve peak performance metrics and maximum ROI.
