Pricing can make or break a quarter. Too aggressive and you leave money on the table; too conservative and you stall deals. AI helps sales leaders navigate this tradeoff by suggesting data-driven discounts and offers that reflect opportunity risk, competitive pressure, budget signals, and historical win patterns. When implemented with clear guardrails and approvals, dynamic pricing boosts both win rate and gross margin while reducing friction between sales and finance.

üì• Inputs for Smart Price Recommendations

  • Deal metadata: segment, region, product mix, ARR/ACV, term length, procurement stage.
  • Competitive context: known competitor, price match requests, feature parity, proof-of-value results.
  • Engagement strength: multi-threading, exec sponsor, proposal interactions, meeting cadence.
  • Historical outcomes: discount-to-win curves by segment and region, seasonality, EOQ pressure.
  • Finance policies: floor prices, approval matrices, nonstandard T&C thresholds.

üß† Model Approaches

  • Uplift models: estimate marginal lift in win probability for each discount step to find the optimal point.
  • Bandit algorithms: adaptively test bundles and incentives (free months, onboarding credits) by segment.
  • Elasticity curves: non-linear relationships between price, term, and win rate for different ICPs.
