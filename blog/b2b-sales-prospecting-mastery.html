üîç The RESEARCH Framework

Effective prospecting starts with systematic research. Our RESEARCH framework ensures you find and qualify the right prospects:

üéØ Real-time Company Intelligence

Use tools like LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo, and Apollo to gather company insights, recent news, and growth indicators.

üìä Evaluate Business Triggers

Identify expansion plans, funding rounds, leadership changes, or technology adoptions that create buying opportunities.

üë§ Stakeholder Mapping

Map decision makers, influencers, and users. Understand reporting structures and buying committee dynamics.

üí° Engage with Relevant Insights

Develop compelling reasons to reach out based on specific challenges or opportunities you've identified.

üìû Activate Multi-Channel Outreach

Use email, LinkedIn, phone, and video in coordinated sequences that build relationships over time.

üìà Record and Refine

Track response rates, meeting conversion, and deal progression to optimize your approach continuously.

üîÑ Continuous Follow-up

Implement systematic follow-up sequences that maintain visibility without being pushy.

üéØ Handle Objections Proactively

Anticipate common objections and build responses into your outreach messaging and qualification process.

üìß High-Converting Outreach Templates

Email 1: The Industry Insight

"Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] just [specific trigger event]. Based on similar situations I've seen with [industry] companies, this often creates [specific challenge]. I have some insights that might be helpful. Would you be open to a brief conversation?"

LinkedIn Message: The Mutual Connection

"Hi [Name], [Mutual connection] suggested I reach out. We're helping [similar company type] companies achieve [specific outcome]. Worth a brief conversation to see if there's a fit?"
