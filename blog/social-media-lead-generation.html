üí∞ ROI Measurement for Social Lead Generation

Measuring the true ROI of social media lead generation requires understanding both direct and indirect benefits, as well as attributing revenue to social media efforts.

Social Media Attribution Models

First-Touch Attribution

Credits the initial social interaction that started the customer journey. Good for measuring awareness and initial engagement.

Multi-Touch Attribution

Distributes credit across all social touchpoints in the customer journey. Provides comprehensive view of social impact.
