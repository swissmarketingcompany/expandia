‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is B2B social selling?

B2B social selling is the practice of using social media platforms (primarily LinkedIn) to build relationships, engage with prospects, and drive sales opportunities. It involves sharing valuable content, engaging with prospects' posts, and building trust before making sales pitches.

Why is LinkedIn the best platform for B2B social selling?

LinkedIn is ideal for B2B social selling because: 94% of B2B marketers use it as their primary social platform, it has 930+ million professional users, advanced targeting capabilities, built-in CRM features, and decision-makers actively use it for business networking and research.

What are typical B2B social selling results on LinkedIn?

Effective LinkedIn social selling typically achieves: 15-25% connection acceptance rates, 8-15% response rates to messages, 3-8% meeting booking rates, and 25-40% higher close rates compared to cold calling. Top performers can achieve even better results with advanced strategies.

How much time should I spend on LinkedIn social selling daily?

Effective social selling requires 30-60 minutes daily: 15 minutes for content creation and posting, 15-20 minutes for engagement (commenting, liking), 15-20 minutes for prospecting and messaging. Consistency is more important than duration - daily activity trumps sporadic longer sessions.
