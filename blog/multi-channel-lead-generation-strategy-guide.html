üîÑ The Multi-Channel Lead Generation Flywheel

Effective multi-channel strategy creates a self-reinforcing flywheel where each channel amplifies the others:

  1. Awareness: Content and paid ads drive initial visibility
  2. Engagement: Social media and email nurture interest
  3. Consideration: Webinars and demos showcase value
  4. Conversion: Personalized outreach closes the loop
  5. Advocacy: Happy customers become channel amplifiers
