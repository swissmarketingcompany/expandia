‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for in an outbound marketing agency?

Look for proven track record with measurable results, industry expertise, modern technology stack, transparent reporting, strong process methodology, cultural fit, and appropriate pricing models. Evaluate their case studies, client testimonials, and ability to understand your specific market and challenges.

How much do outbound marketing agencies typically cost?

Outbound marketing agency costs vary by service level: Basic lead generation ($3,000-8,000/month), Full-service outbound ($8,000-20,000/month), Enterprise programs ($20,000-50,000/month). Most agencies charge retainer fees plus performance bonuses or commissions ranging from 5-20% of generated revenue.

What's the typical timeline to see results from an outbound marketing agency?

Most reputable outbound marketing agencies deliver initial results within 4-8 weeks: Week 1-2 (setup and strategy), Week 3-4 (campaign launch), Week 5-8 (optimization and scaling). Full optimization typically takes 3-6 months to achieve peak performance and ROI.

How do I evaluate agency proposals and avoid bad choices?

Use a structured evaluation scorecard covering track record, industry expertise, technology, team quality, process methodology, reporting capabilities, and cultural fit. Avoid agencies that guarantee specific results, demand large upfront payments, provide generic proposals, or can't explain their methodology clearly.
