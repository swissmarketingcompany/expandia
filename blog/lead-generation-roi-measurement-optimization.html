üîç Advanced Attribution Modeling

Single-touch attribution is dead. Modern B2B buyers interact with 8-12 touchpoints before purchasing. Implement these attribution models to understand your true ROI:

  • First-Touch Attribution: Credits the initial awareness driver
  • Last-Touch Attribution: Credits the final conversion driver
  • Linear Attribution: Equal credit across all touchpoints
  • Time-Decay Attribution: More credit to recent interactions
  • Position-Based Attribution: Emphasizes first and last touches
  • Data-Driven Attribution: Machine learning-based weighting
