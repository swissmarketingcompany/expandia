What Do We Do?

We provide end-to-end email marketing management, from strategic planning and content creation to list hygiene and performance tracking, ensuring every aspect of your email program drives business growth.

ü§ñ

Automated Emails (Flows)

Powerful way to generate revenue on autopilot. Triggered by customer behavior, they engage your audience with personalized content at the perfect time.

Welcome Series

To onboard new distributors, suppliers, or large-scale clients. This series introduces your company's values, key contacts, support resources, and outlines the benefits of partnering with you.

Inventory Alert / Product Update

To proactively inform your B2B partners about critical changes in your supply chain. This flow triggers emails when a new product is available, stock levels are low on a popular item, or a product specification has been updated.

Quote Follow-Up

To follow up with potential clients after they've requested a quote or received a proposal. This flow provides additional valuable information, clarifies common questions, and subtly encourages them to move forward.

Post-Purchase

To confirm a successful order and its delivery. This flow is critical for building trust and a positive brand experience after a transaction. It can also be used to proactively offer support, share product usage guides, or prompt a review.

Customer Win-Back & More

Advanced flows including abandoned cart recovery, browse abandonment, replenishment reminders, customer milestones, upsell/cross-sell sequences, and lead nurturing campaigns.

üìß

One-Off Emails (Campaigns)

Single-send messages used to deliver timely news, promotions, or announcements. They are essential for driving immediate sales and engaging your audience.

Innovation Launch

To officially announce a significant new product line, innovative technology, or advanced material to your network. Generate immediate interest, drive traffic to detailed product resources, and prompt direct inquiries.

Compliance & Regulatory Update

To proactively communicate changes in European regulations, certifications (e.g., CE marking, ISO standards), or product compliance to your partners. Builds trust and positions your company as a reliable industry leader.

Newsletters

A simple yet powerful way to keep partners informed and engaged. Share company news, major project highlights, team spotlights, and CSR initiatives to build stronger brand identity.

Special Promotions & Events

Limited-time offers, product launches, holiday campaigns, event invitations, milestone celebrations, and educational content to drive immediate engagement and sales.

Customer Stories & Behind the Scenes

Feature case studies, testimonials, team spotlights, and exclusive behind-the-scenes content to build social proof, trust, and personal connections with your audience.
