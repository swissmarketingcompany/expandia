Common Questions

Transparency is our core value. Here is how we build your revenue engine.

01.

What CRM platforms do you support?

We focus on the "Big Three": HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive. We don't just "install" them; we architect them specifically for your sales velocity. We help you choose the tier that fits your budget without overpaying for features you won't use.

02.

Can you move our existing data?

Yes. Migration is the most dangerous part of a RevOps setup. We handle the full export, cleaning, and mapping of your existing contacts and deals. We ensure your historical data is preserved and actually usable in the new automated workflows.

03.

What automations are included?

We build "The Big Four": Automated Lead Scoring, Multi-channel Sequences, Slack/Team Notifications for sales actions, and Auto-reporting Dashboards. Your team stops doing manual entry and starts doing manual closing.

04.

Do you connect with our other tools?

RevOps doesn't live in a bubble. we connect your CRM to your entire stack: LinkedIn, specialized prospecting tools, calendar apps, and accounting software. One truth, across every platform.
