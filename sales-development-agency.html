Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide the leads?

No. We handle list building using our proprietary databases and premium tools like ZoomInfo and Apollo. However, we're happy to target specific accounts you provide (ABM).

Is this a replacement for my internal team?

It can be. Many clients use us as their entire outbound engine, while others use us to support or compete with their internal SDRs. We integrate seamlessly with your existing structure.

What happens when a meeting is booked?

We book the meeting directly onto your Account Executives' calendars. We ensure the prospect shows up by handling confirmations and reminders.

How long are the contracts?

We operate on a 3-month initial pilot to prove ROI, followed by flexible month-to-month or quarterly terms. We believe we should re-earn your business every month.
