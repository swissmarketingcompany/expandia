Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI match my brand style?

You upload your brand kit (logo, color palette, fonts, and 3-5 example designs). Our AI analyzes these and generates graphics that match your visual identity.

Can I request changes to the designs?

Yes! All graphics are delivered in editable formats (Canva, Figma, or PSD). You can tweak colors, text, or layouts anytime. Pro and Enterprise plans include revision support.

What social platforms do you support?

We support Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, and Pinterest. Graphics are optimized for each platform's dimensions and best practices.

Do I own the graphics?

Yes. You have full commercial rights to all graphics. Use them forever, repurpose them, or even resell them if you want.

What if I need more graphics mid-year?

You can purchase add-on packs (30, 60, or 90 graphics) at any time. We'll match your existing style and schedule them into your calendar.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day review period. If you're not satisfied with the first 10 graphics, we'll refund you in full or redesign them to your specifications.
