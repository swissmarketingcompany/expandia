‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is CPQ software and how does it work?

CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) software automates the sales proposal process by allowing sales teams to configure products/services, calculate accurate pricing based on rules and discounts, and generate professional quotes instantly. It integrates with CRM systems and streamlines complex pricing scenarios, reducing errors and accelerating deal closure.

What are the main benefits of using CPQ software?

Key benefits include: 50-80% faster quote generation, 95%+ pricing accuracy, 25-40% increase in deal closure rates, reduced administrative burden on sales teams, consistent pricing and margins, improved customer experience with faster response times, and better sales analytics and reporting capabilities.

How do I choose the right CPQ software for my business?

Consider: Product complexity (simple vs. highly configurable), pricing complexity (standard vs. dynamic pricing), integration requirements (CRM, ERP, billing systems), user volume and roles, customization needs, implementation timeline, budget, vendor support quality, and scalability requirements for future growth.

How long does CPQ implementation typically take?

Implementation timelines vary by complexity: Simple CPQ (1-3 months), Standard implementation (3-6 months), Complex enterprise deployment (6-12 months), Custom solutions (12+ months). Factors include product complexity, integration requirements, data migration needs, and customization level.
