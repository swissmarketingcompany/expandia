2. Subject Line Psychology & Testing

Subject lines determine whether your carefully crafted emails ever get opened. The highest-performing email subject lines for lead generation combine curiosity, urgency, and personalization.

Curiosity-Driven

  • "The [Industry] secret everyone's talking about"
  • "Why [Company] chose us over [Competitor]"
  • "[Name], this might surprise you"

Value-Focused

  • "5 ways to increase [metric] by 40%"
  • "Free [valuable resource] inside"
  • "[Name], your [industry] report is ready"

Urgency & Scarcity

  • "Last chance: [Offer] expires tonight"
  • "Only 3 spots left for [Event]"
  • "[Name], don't miss this deadline"
