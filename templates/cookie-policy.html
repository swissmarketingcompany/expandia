Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Go Expandia uses cookies and similar technologies on our website for site operation, analytics, form handling, chat, and marketing measurement.

Last updated: May 21, 2026

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on a browser or device. Similar technologies include pixels, local storage, tags, scripts, and identifiers used to operate a website, remember settings, measure usage, and connect form or chat activity.

How We Use Cookies

  • Necessary cookies: support security, page loading, form submission, spam prevention, and core website functions.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand page visits, traffic sources, device types, navigation, and content performance.
  • Functional cookies: support live chat, embedded forms, preferences, and improved user experience.
  • Marketing cookies: help measure campaigns, form journeys, and follow-up activity when permitted.

Tools We Use

The website may use Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, HubSpot, Tawk.to, and similar operational or analytics tools. These tools can collect technical data such as page URL, referrer, browser, device, approximate location, interaction events, and identifiers.

We use these tools to improve the site, respond to inquiries, understand which content is useful, diagnose issues, and manage business communications.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies. Blocking some cookies may affect forms, chat, analytics opt-outs, or other website features.

You can also use privacy tools and provider-level controls where available, including browser-level "Do Not Track" or global privacy signals. Some third-party tools may not respond to every signal in the same way.

Third-Party Processing

Third-party providers may set or read cookies when their scripts load on our site. Their processing is governed by their own policies and settings as well as our configuration.

Relationship to the Privacy Policy

Cookies can involve personal data where identifiers or usage data relate to a person or device. More detail about data handling, retention, rights, and contact details is available in our Privacy Policy.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as our website, tools, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of the page shows the latest revision.
