1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website expandia.ch. They help us provide you with a better user experience and allow our website to function properly.

Cookies contain information about your preferences and activities on our website, but they do not contain personal information that can directly identify you unless you choose to provide it.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for our website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and core website features.

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to work.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and if visitors get error messages from web pages.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - User identification 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - Session tracking 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - User identification 24 hours

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features.

  • Language preferences and regional settings
  • Theme and display preferences
  • User interface customizations
  • Form auto-fill and saved preferences

3.4 Marketing/Targeting Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They may be set by our advertising partners through our site.

You can opt out of these cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can choose which types of cookies to accept. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Preferences" link in our footer.

5.2 Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your web browser settings. Here's how to do it in major browsers:

🌐 Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

🦊 Firefox

Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

🧭 Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

📱 Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.

6. Cookie Duration

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain your session while navigating our website.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a specified period (ranging from 24 hours to 2 years) and help us remember your preferences for future visits.
