Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our lead generation service

How quickly can we start seeing results?

Most clients see their first qualified leads within 2-3 weeks of campaign launch. Full campaign optimization typically occurs within 4-6 weeks, after which you'll see consistent, predictable lead flow.

What makes your leads higher quality than competitors?

We use a multi-layer qualification process including firmographic data, behavioral signals, and human verification. Every lead is scored based on budget, authority, need, and timeline (BANT) before delivery to your team.

Do you guarantee a minimum number of leads?

Yes, we guarantee the minimum lead volume specified in your package. If we don't deliver the promised number of qualified leads, we'll extend your campaign at no additional cost until we meet the commitment.

How do you ensure GDPR compliance?

All our outreach campaigns are fully GDPR compliant. We maintain detailed opt-in records, provide easy unsubscribe options, and only contact prospects who have given proper consent or where we have legitimate business interest.

Can you integrate with our existing CRM?

Absolutely. We integrate with all major CRMs including Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and many others. Leads are automatically synced to your system with all relevant data and interaction history.
