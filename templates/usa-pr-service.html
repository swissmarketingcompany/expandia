Frequently Asked Questions

Can I distribute PR from outside the US?

Yes. Any company interested in the US market can use this service. It's especially effective for companies with US presence.

Which press distribution platforms do you work with?

We work with major US media players. Coverage varies by chosen package (local, regional, national).

How many publications do you guarantee?

We don't guarantee a specific number of publications. We guarantee platform distribution and publication report. Publications depend on news quality and timing. Platforms decide editorial pickup independently.

Will it appear in my preferred outlet?

We don't guarantee specific outlets. Press services distribute to news agencies and outlets, which publish based on their own criteria.

Is there a minimum number of releases?

No. We can work on a single release. For regular campaigns, we can offer package pricing.

Do you write the text?

Yes. We take your news and materials and write in professional media format. We present it for your approval and revise as needed.
