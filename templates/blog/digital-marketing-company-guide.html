üîç How to Evaluate Digital Marketing Companies

Evaluation Framework & Criteria

Selecting the right digital marketing partner requires systematic evaluation of capabilities, experience, cultural fit, and performance track record. A structured approach helps avoid costly mistakes and ensures optimal partnership outcomes.

1. Portfolio & Case Study Review

Examine previous work, results achieved, and industry experience relevance.

2. Team Expertise Assessment

Evaluate team qualifications, certifications, and experience levels.

3. Process & Methodology Review

Understand their approach to strategy, execution, and optimization.

4. Communication & Reporting

Assess reporting frequency, transparency, and account management style.

5. Cultural & Strategic Alignment

Ensure shared values, communication style, and business philosophy fit.
