Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AI avatar video?

An AI avatar is a digital presenter that speaks your script. It looks and sounds like a real person, but it's generated entirely by AI. No filming, no actors, no studio needed.

Can I use my own voice or face?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include custom avatar creation. We can clone your voice and create a digital twin of you (with your permission, of course).

What video platforms do you support?

We support YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn, and Facebook. Videos are optimized for each platform's format (vertical, square, or horizontal).

Can I edit the videos after delivery?

Yes. All videos are delivered in MP4 format with editable project files (if requested). You can tweak text, music, or visuals anytime.

Do I own the videos?

Yes. You have full commercial rights to all videos. Use them forever, repurpose them, or even resell them if you want.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day review period. If you're not satisfied with the first 10 videos, we'll refund you in full or remake them to your specifications.
