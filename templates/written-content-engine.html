Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI know what to write about?

You provide us with your industry, target keywords, and brand voice guidelines. Our AI analyzes your existing content (if any) and generates a year-long editorial calendar tailored to your business.

Can I review the content before it's published?

Yes! All content is delivered to you in a CSV/Google Sheets format. You can review, edit, or approve before we schedule it. Or, you can trust the AI and let it auto-publish.

What platforms do you support for auto-publishing?

We support WordPress, Medium, LinkedIn, and custom blog platforms via API. If you need a different platform, we can usually integrate it.

Is the content truly SEO-optimized?

Yes. Each article includes target keywords, meta descriptions, internal linking suggestions, and follows SEO best practices for readability and structure.

What if I want to change the schedule later?

You own the content. You can reschedule, edit, or delete posts anytime through your publishing platform's dashboard.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day review period. If you're not satisfied with the content quality after reviewing the first 10 articles, we'll refund you in full.
