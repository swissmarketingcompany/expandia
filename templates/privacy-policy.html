Information We Collect

Project and AI Data

During AI consulting, automation, agent, or custom AI solution work, clients may share business materials such as workflows, documents, CRM exports, operational records, customer service examples, reports, and system descriptions. We use this information only to scope, deliver, support, and improve the agreed work.

We do not use client project data to train public AI models unless a written agreement explicitly allows it. Where practical, we limit, mask, or remove private personal data and sensitive details before analysis or implementation work. Client materials remain subject to the confidentiality, data-processing, and security terms agreed for the project.
