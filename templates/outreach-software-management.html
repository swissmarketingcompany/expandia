Frequently Asked Questions

Which tools do you recommend?

It depends on your volume. For high-volume cold email, we recommend Smartlead or Instantly. For detailed account-based marketing, Clay or Apollo is better. We help you choose and set up the right one.

Do I need to buy the software myself?

Yes, you own your accounts. We guide you on which subscription tier to buy so you don't overspend, but the account remains 100% yours.

How long does it take to fix my spam issues?

Technical fixes (DNS) are done in 24 hours. Domain reputation repair can take 2-4 weeks depending on how severe the damage is. We can set up fresh domains to get you sending immediately while the old ones heal.
