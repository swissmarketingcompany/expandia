Why Berlin teams use AI

Berlin has a strong startup, SaaS, media, mobility, ecommerce, professional services, and international operations base. Many teams already experiment with AI, but the gap is turning personal prompting into repeatable company workflows that are secure and maintainable.

Privacy, access control, and clear process ownership matter. The best projects make AI helpful without creating uncontrolled shadow systems.
