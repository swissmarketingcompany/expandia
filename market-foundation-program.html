What's Included

A comprehensive, 3-phase system designed to make you look like a native player from Day 1.

1
Phase 1

Digital Real Estate & Infrastructure

Creating the "Virtual HQ" so you look like a verified local entity.

  • Local Domain ProcurementRegion-specific domains (.ch, .de, .co.uk) to bypass filters & build trust.
  • Workspace Structure10 Google Workspaces (partners@, accounts@) for department verification.
  • Local PresenceLocal phone number provisioning and "Virtual Office" footprint.
  • Technical ComplianceFull DNS/DKIM/DMARC authentication for enterprise deliverability.
2
Phase 2

Localization & Positioning

Translating your value proposition into the local commercial dialect.

  • Competitor MappingAnalyzing incumbents to identify the specific market gap for your brand.
  • Cultural AdaptationRewriting messaging for local business etiquette (localization, not translation).
  • Persona DevelopmentCreating profiles for "Regional Managers" to appear as senior consultants.
  • Asset LocalizationAdapting sales collateral and signatures for the target region.
3
Phase 3

Territory Development

Executing the go-to-market strategy. The Outreach.

  • Ecosystem OutreachCampaigns targeting partners and multipliers, not just end-clients.
  • Multi-Channel TouchpointsEmail scripts + LinkedIn actions as a unified "Business Development" effort.
  • Meeting LogisticsHandling all back-and-forth scheduling to book firm calendar slots.
  • Market Feedback LoopWeekly reports on market sentiment and product-market fit data.
