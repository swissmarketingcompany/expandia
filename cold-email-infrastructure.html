Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about cold email infrastructure.

What is Cold Email?

Cold email is a proactive outreach strategy where you send personalized emails to potential B2B clients who haven't interacted with your brand before. Unlike spam, it's targeted, value-driven, and designed to start a business conversation rather than make an immediate sale.

What is Cold Email Infrastructure?

It's a system of multiple domains and email accounts configured with strict technical records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) to send large volumes of cold emails without landing in spam folders.

Why do I need 10+ inboxes?

Sending too many emails from one account triggers spam filters. Distributing volume across multiple inboxes (e.g., sending 30-50 emails per day per inbox) keeps your sender reputation high and ensures 99%+ deliverability.

Do you provide the leads?

Yes, our packages include verified lead credits to get you started. We also offer a separate Verified Lead List service if you need more volume or specific targeting.

How long does setup take?

We guarantee a 48-hour turnaround for the technical setup. The automated "warmup" period takes about 14 days to gradually build reputation before you can send at full volume. You own the system forever.
