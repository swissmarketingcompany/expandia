Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our outreach software management services.

Which tools do you recommend?

It depends on your volume and strategy. Our recommendations include:

  • High-volume cold email: Smartlead or Instantly for maximum sending capacity
  • Account-based marketing: Clay or Apollo for targeted, personalized outreach
  • Multi-channel sequences: Lemlist for email + LinkedIn combined workflows
  • Enterprise sales: Outreach or Salesloft for complex deal cycles
  • Data enrichment: ZoomInfo or Lusha for verified contact information

We help you choose and set up the right combination based on your goals and budget.

Do I need to buy the software myself?

Yes, you own your accounts. This ensures full data ownership and continuity. Our role includes:

  • Subscription guidance: We advise on which tier to buy so you don't overspend
  • Full configuration: We handle all setup, integrations, and optimizations
  • Ongoing management: We monitor and maintain everything daily
  • Account ownership: Your accounts remain 100% yours at all times

How long does it take to fix my spam issues?

The timeline depends on the severity of the damage:

Issue Fix Time Solution
DNS Records (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) 24 Hours Immediate technical fix
Domain Reputation Repair 2-4 Weeks Gradual warmup & reputation rebuild
Fresh Domain Setup 48 Hours New domains to send immediately

We can set up fresh domains to get you sending immediately while the old ones heal.
